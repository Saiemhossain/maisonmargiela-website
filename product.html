    Accessories

    Accessories are a key element of the Maison Margiela aesthetic. Conceived by creative director John
    Galliano, these seasonal proposals include small leather goods such as wallets, coin purses, cell phone
    holders, card holders and key rings. Among the leather and knitted accessories, belts, gloves and scarves
    stand out, while the hats range from caps to masks. Rings, earrings, bracelets, cufflinks and necklaces made
    of metal with a gold or silver finish: a means of stylistic expression, the accessories are permeated by the
    codes and distinctive characteristics of the Maison Margiela, from the white four stitches to the numeric logo
    and the whitewash effect in paint.

    The Parisian fashion house Maison Margiela bases its codes on the concept of non-
    conformism and subversion of norms. Appointed Creative Director in 2014, British
    designer John Galliano uses his own visual language to broaden its values, creating a
    new technical vocabulary that consolidates Maison Margiela's status as a singular and
    autonomous entity in the world of luxury.
